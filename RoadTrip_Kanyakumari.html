4-Day Road Trip Bangalore to Kanyakumari Itinerary

Bangalore → Kanyakumari → Rameshwaram → Madurai → Thanjavur → Bangalore

Trip plan by Sumanth Bharadwaj H S

Day 1: Bangalore → Kanyakumari

Distance: ~700 km | Drive Time: ~10–11 hours

  • 6:00 AM: Depart from Bangalore (New Tippasandra) early to avoid city traffic.
  • Follow NH 44 towards Salem/Madurai, then continue south to Kanyakumari.
  • Take short breaks every 2–3 hours (e.g., Hosur/Salem for breakfast, Tirunelveli for lunch).
  • 4:00–5:00 PM: Arrive at Kanyakumari, check into your hotel.
  • Catch the sunset (usually around 6:00 PM) at the beach viewpoint.
  • Overnight stay in Kanyakumari.

Day 2: Explore Kanyakumari → Rameshwaram

Distance: ~310 km | Drive Time: ~6 hours

  • 6:00 AM: Optional sunrise viewing at Kanyakumari beach.
  • 7:30–8:00 AM: Ferry to Vivekananda Rock Memorial. Admire Thiruvalluvar Statue from the ferry.
  • Lunch around 12:30–1:00 PM in Kanyakumari.
  • 2:00 PM: Depart for Rameshwaram (~6 hours drive).
  • 8:00–8:30 PM: Arrive in Rameshwaram, hotel check‑in.
  • Overnight stay in Rameshwaram.

Day 3: Rameshwaram → Madurai → Thanjavur

Segment 1 (Rameshwaram → Madurai): ~170 km (3–4 hrs)
Segment 2 (Madurai → Thanjavur): ~190 km (3.5–4 hrs)

  • 6:00–7:30 AM: Darshan at Ramanathaswamy Temple.
  • (Optional) If visiting Dhanushkodi, start extra early (~2-hour round trip).
  • 9:00–9:30 AM: Depart Rameshwaram for Madurai.
  • 1:00–2:00 PM: Arrive in Madurai, quick lunch near temple.
  • 2:00–3:30 PM: Visit Meenakshi Amman Temple. (Queue times can vary.)
  • 4:00 PM: Depart Madurai for Thanjavur (~3.5–4 hrs drive).
  • 8:00–9:00 PM: Arrive in Thanjavur, hotel check‑in.
  • Overnight stay in Thanjavur.

Day 4: Thanjavur → Bangalore

Distance: ~400–420 km | Drive Time: ~7–8 hours

  • 7:00–8:30 AM: Visit Brihadeeswarar Temple (UNESCO World Heritage Site).
  • 9:00 AM: Depart Thanjavur via Trichy & Salem toward Bangalore.
  • 5:00–6:00 PM: Estimated arrival in Bangalore (depending on traffic/breaks).
  • Trip concludes.

TIPS & NOTES:
  • Temple Timings: Meenakshi & Ramanathaswamy can have long queues. Early visits or special-darshan tickets can help.
  • Food: Plenty of highway dhabas & restaurants (A2B, Murugan Idli, etc.).
  • Dress Code: Major temples require modest attire; men may need to remove shirts in some sanctums.
  • No Night Driving: Aim to wrap up drives by 9–10 PM. Start days early for comfortable travel.

Things to Carry & Pre-Travel Checklist

1. Essential Documents & Info

  • Valid Driver’s License & Photo ID
  • Car Insurance & Registration Papers
  • Any Temple Tickets or booking receipts
  • Emergency contact numbers & roadside assistance details

2. Vehicle Prep (Diesel Car)

  • Fuel up (full tank) before departure
  • Check engine oil, coolant, brake fluid, power steering fluid
  • Inspect tire pressure & tread (including spare tire)
  • Carry basic tool kit, jack, wheel spanner, jump-start cables
  • Ensure car battery and lights are functioning properly

3. Personal Essentials

  • Comfortable clothing & footwear (consider temple dress codes)
  • Medicines (any prescription meds, first-aid kit, pain relievers)
  • Sunglasses, sunscreen (coastal regions can be hot)
  • Hand sanitizer, tissues/wet wipes
  • Reusable water bottles or bottled water supply
  • Snacks (energy bars, fruits, nuts) for long stretches

4. Electronics & Gadgets

  • Smartphone & chargers (car charger, power bank)
  • Optional: GPS device or offline maps (in case of patchy network)
  • Camera with extra batteries/memory cards (if desired)

5. Pre-Travel Tasks

  • Service your car at least a week before (if due)
  • Recharge FASTAG for seamless toll experience
  • Book hotels in Kanyakumari, Rameshwaram, Thanjavur in advance
  • Check temple timings & any special puja schedules
  • Plan an early morning departure to avoid city traffic
  • Keep small change for tolls/parking

Safe Travels & Happy Exploring! -- Sumanth Bharadwaj H S
