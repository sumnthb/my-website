Welcome to the Math Club

Igniting curiosity and fostering a love for mathematics

About Our Club

The Math Club is a community of enthusiasts who believe in the power of numbers, problem-solving, and logical thinking. We organize weekly meetings, problem-solving sessions, and competitions to help students deepen their understanding and appreciation of mathematics.

Activities & Events

Explore the variety of events we host throughout the semester

Weekly Problem-Solving Sessions

Sharpen your problem-solving skills by tackling interesting and challenging puzzles every week, guided by experienced mentors and fellow math enthusiasts.

Guest Lectures

Learn from renowned mathematicians and professionals who share their research insights, career paths, and inspirations in the world of mathematics.

Math Competitions

Take part in local and international math contests like the Putnam Exam, and represent our club as we celebrate the spirit of competition and collaboration.

Social Gatherings

Because learning is also about having fun, we organize social gatherings and game nights to bring our members closer together.
